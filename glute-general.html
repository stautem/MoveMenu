Glute General

Goal: weight loss + glutes & legs. Full equipment access.

General guidance only, not medical advice. Warm up before lifting, use a weight you can control for all reps, and stop any movement that causes pain.

Squat

  • Barbell Back Squat

    • Bar across upper back, feet shoulder-width
    • Sit hips back and down, chest up
    • Drive through mid-foot to stand

  • Goblet Squat

    • Hold dumbbell vertically at chest
    • Squat down keeping elbows inside knees
    • Stand tall, squeeze glutes at top

  • Leg Press

    • Feet shoulder-width on platform
    • Lower until knees hit ~90°
    • Press through heels, don't lock knees hard

  • Barbell Front Squat

    • Bar rests on front shoulders, elbows high
    • Squat down staying upright
    • Drive up through the whole foot

Hip Hinge

  • Barbell Deadlift

    • Bar over mid-foot, grip just outside knees
    • Flat back, push floor away to stand
    • Hips and shoulders rise together

  • Romanian Deadlift

    • Soft knees, hinge hips back
    • Bar stays close to legs
    • Feel a stretch in hamstrings, then stand

  • Kettlebell Swing

    • Hinge and hike bell back between legs
    • Snap hips forward to drive it up
    • Arms just guide, hips do the work

  • Cable Pull-Through

    • Face away from low cable, rope between legs
    • Hinge forward, then squeeze glutes to stand

Glutes

  • Barbell Hip Thrust

    • Upper back on bench, bar over hips
    • Drive hips up until body is flat
    • Squeeze glutes hard at the top

  • Cable Glute Kickback

    • Ankle cuff on low cable
    • Kick leg straight back, squeeze glute
    • Control the return

  • Hip Abduction Machine

    • Sit tall, push knees outward against pads
    • Slow, controlled return

  • Step-Up

    • Step onto box, drive through the lead heel
    • Stand tall at top before stepping down

Lunge / Unilateral

  • Walking Lunge

    • Step forward, drop back knee toward floor
    • Push through front heel to the next step

  • Reverse Lunge

    • Step backward instead of forward
    • Easier on the knees than walking lunges

  • Bulgarian Split Squat

    • Rear foot up on a bench
    • Lower straight down on the front leg

Push

  • Barbell Bench Press

    • Grip slightly wider than shoulders
    • Lower to chest, press up over shoulders

  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press

    • Press dumbbells overhead from shoulder height
    • Avoid arching the lower back

  • Push-Up

    • Hands under shoulders, body in a straight line
    • Lower chest to floor, press back up

Pull

  • Lat Pulldown

    • Pull bar to upper chest
    • Lead with elbows, squeeze shoulder blades

  • Seated Cable Row

    • Sit tall, pull handle to torso
    • Squeeze shoulder blades together

  • Barbell Row

    • Hinge forward, flat back
    • Pull bar to lower ribs

Core

  • Plank

    • Forearms and toes on floor, body straight
    • Hold, don't let hips sag

  • Cable Woodchop

    • Rotate cable diagonally across body
    • Control the return, don't just let it snap back

  • Hanging Leg Raise

    • Hang from bar, raise knees toward chest
    • Lower with control
