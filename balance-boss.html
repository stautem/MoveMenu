Plan B — Balance & Strength

Goal: strength & balance training, doctor-recommended. Built around the 7 basic movement patterns.

General guidance only, not medical advice. Follow your doctor's or physical therapist's specific instructions, keep a chair or sturdy surface nearby for balance work, and stop any movement that causes pain.

Squat

  • Sit-to-Stand (Chair Squat)

    • Stand up from a chair without using hands if possible
    • Sit back down slowly with control

  • Light Goblet Squat

    • Hold a light dumbbell at chest
    • Squat only as low as comfortable, keep chest up

  • Seated Leg Extension (light)

    • Use the leg extension machine, light weight
    • Straighten knee slowly, lower with control

Hip Hinge

  • Hip Hinge Drill

    • Hold a stick along your back (head, upper back, tailbone touching)
    • Push hips back, keep the stick in contact at all 3 points

  • Light Dumbbell Romanian Deadlift

    • Hold light dumbbells, soft knees
    • Hinge hips back, lower only to a comfortable range

Push

  • Wall Push-Up

    • Hands on wall at shoulder height
    • Lower chest toward wall, push back

  • Seated Dumbbell Chest Press

    • Light dumbbells, press forward from chest height

  • Chest Press Machine (light)

    • Adjust seat so handles line up with chest
    • Press out, control the return

Pull

  • Seated Cable Row (light)

    • Sit tall, pull handle to torso
    • Squeeze shoulder blades together

  • Resistance Band Row

    • Anchor band at chest height
    • Pull elbows back, squeeze shoulder blades

  • Lat Pulldown (light)

    • Pull bar to upper chest, lead with elbows

Carry

  • Farmer's Carry

    • Hold a dumbbell in each hand
    • Walk a set distance with tall posture, shoulders back

  • Unilateral (Suitcase) Carry

    • Hold one dumbbell in a single hand
    • Walk without leaning to either side
    • Repeat on the other side

Lunge

  • Assisted Split Stance Sit-to-Stand

    • One foot forward, one back, near a chair or rail
    • Bend and straighten the front knee, hold support as needed

  • Low Step-Up

    • Step up onto the low box, near a rail for balance
    • Step down with control

Rotation / Anti-Rotation

  • Band Pallof Press

    • Band anchored to the side at chest height
    • Press handle straight out, resist the band pulling you sideways

  • Seated Trunk Rotation (light)

    • Sit tall holding a light weight or ball
    • Rotate slowly side to side within a comfortable range

Balance

  • Single-Leg Stance

    • Hold a chair or rail
    • Lift one foot slightly, hold, switch sides

  • Heel-to-Toe Walk

    • Walk in a straight line placing heel directly in front of toes
    • Stay near a wall for support if needed

  • Standing Marches

    • Hold support, lift knees one at a time to hip height
